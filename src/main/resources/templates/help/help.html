Help

To ask for help write an email to skillsharinghelp@gmail.com and our administrators will be happy to answer any questions you may have.

With SkillSharing you can do mainly 3 things:

  • Propose a collaboration, in which you offer to tutor someone about a certain skill.

  • Request a collaboration, in which you ask for help for a certain skill.

  • Collaborate, that is to accept an already posted Proposal or Request to start a collaboration with someone.

Here are some tips in order to use SkillSharing efficiently:

  • You can see your hour balance in your profile, this is an application made for seeking help and giving help, if you don't balance both the admnins will ban you!

  • Before making a proposal or request see if there is someone already in the list to make a collaboration, maybe someone is already offering what you need!

  • You can finalize the Collaborations in your personal menu, that way you can also assign how many hours you and your companion made.
